Bungalows at Americana

Why Bungalows over the competition?
At Americana, our bungalows provide the seclusion and privacy with the ease of accessibility to a beach that a chain-hotel cant that is convienient to our clients. Enjoy a cold coconut and sip within the space of your bungalow today!
What comes in each bungalow?
All bungalows at Americana come furnished with two beds and a outdoor shower. Toiletries and towels are provided during the duration of your stay with us. On-call maid staff is avaialable throughout the day and evening, maid service begins check-ups at 9:30am M-F.
What should I wear during my stay?
Florida is a humid, subtropical region of the United States. From May to October the Miami area enters a rainy season, we provide our guests 2 giant beach umbrellas to shield our clients from the rain! Any other period the area is hot and tropical so shorts and flip-flops are a must!
