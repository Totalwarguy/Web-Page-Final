About Americana

Business Founder
I, Matthew Chumsena, came up with this business after the history of living in the midwest for so long and dealing with the bipolar weather here in Kansas. I hatched up this business of Americana while spending my vacation with my grandparents in Texas.
Foundations
In that brief period I recognized how stable the weather was and how warm the climates that the southern United States enjoyed. I packed up my things my purchased a location in the Miami area too captialize on the opportunity
What should I wear during my stay?
Florida is a humid, subtropical region of the United States. From May to October the Miami area enters a rainy season, we provide our guests 2 giant beach umbrellas to shield our clients from the rain! Any other period the area is hot and tropical so shorts and flip-flops are a must!
